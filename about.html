About Us

Football Game

American football is a team sport played with an oval-shaped ball on a rectangular field.
The objective is to score more points than the opposing team by advancing the ball into the opponent's end zone.
This can be achieved by running with the ball, passing it to a teammate, or kicking it through the goalposts.

Vision

Vision Football is a coach-led initiative. Our objective is to develop players through guided self-discovery, enabling them to think and play for approach to the game.
Our coaching staff has developed the entire curriculum keeping the above objective in mind. we strive to give players maximum match and tournament exposure, which we believe is a crucial aspect in developing a player.
Our holistic program focusses on fitness, skills, mental conditioning and match practice.

History

Modern football originated in Britain in the 19th century. Since before medieval times, “folk football” games had been played in towns and villages according to local customs and with a minimum of rules.
Industrialization and urbanization, which reduced the amount of leisure time and space available to the working class, combined with a history of legal prohibitions
against particularly violent and destructive forms of folk football to undermine the games status from the early 19th century onward.
